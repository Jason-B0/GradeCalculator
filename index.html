Course Grade Calculator

The Dalhousie Course Grade Calculator is a user-friendly web tool designed to assist students in calculating their course grades quickly and accurately. With intuitive input fields for item names, types, marks, and weights, users can easily add and manage their course components. The calculator provides real-time updates on the current grade based on user inputs, empowering students to monitor their academic progress effectively. Whether preparing for upcoming assessments or tracking overall performance, this calculator streamlines the grade calculation process, helping students make informed decisions to achieve their academic goals.
